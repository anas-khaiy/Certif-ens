\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

The digital transformation of higher education introduces critical challenges regarding assessment integrity and pedagogical efficiency. This project presents \textbf{CertifEns}, an intelligent ecosystem designed to unify certification management, automated proctoring, and structured supervision. To address the lack of trust in remote evaluations and the burden of content creation, we designed a microservices architecture based on Spring Boot and React, integrated with state-of-the-art AI models. The solution leverages YOLOv8 and MediaPipe for real-time automated proctoring, while incorporating Ollama for the automated generation of educational content and interactive quizzes. The platform also offers virtual meeting rooms and a collaborative task management system to enhance trainer-learner interactions. This work explores how the synergy between computer vision and Large Language Models can redefine the standards of online certification and digital pedagogical follow-up.

\vspace{1cm}

\noindent \textbf{Keywords:} AI Proctoring, Ollama, Microservices, YOLOv8, MediaPipe.
